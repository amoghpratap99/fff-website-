// our mission

Real money skills,
for real life.

Most teens graduate high school without ever learning how to budget, invest, or build credit. We're here to change that — through engaging, accessible programs designed by the people who need them most.

🚀

Empower

Equip teens with the financial knowledge traditional classrooms overlook.

💸

Educate

Deliver hands-on workshops, games, and tools that make learning stick.

✨

Elevate

Shape the next generation of financially confident young adults.

🎯

Engage

Build a community where teens learn, grow, and lead together.

// what we do

Our programs.

Three core pillars that take financial literacy from textbook theory to real-world skill.

01 / WORKSHOPS

Interactive Workshops

Multi-day sessions covering the financial fundamentals every teen should know before adulthood.

  • Budgeting & saving
  • Investing 101
  • Credit & debt
  • Taxes made simple
02 / COMPETITION

Pitch Competitions

Students develop real business ideas, build pitch decks, and present to a panel of judges — winning cash prizes and mentorship.

  • Business model design
  • Public speaking
  • Financial projections
  • Real prizes & mentorship
03 / COMMUNITY

Outreach & Community

Free resources, school partnerships, and a peer network that turns financial literacy into a movement, not a chore.

  • School chapter program
  • Free digital toolkits
  • Speaker & mentor network
  • Year-round events
